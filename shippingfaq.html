Shipping FAQ

Answers to your shipping questions

What shipping methods do you offer?
We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days), express shipping (2-3 business days), and overnight shipping (1 business day) for domestic orders. International shipping options vary by destination.
How much does shipping cost?
Shipping costs vary based on the shipping method and destination. Standard shipping is free for orders over $50. You can view exact shipping costs at checkout before completing your purchase.
How long will it take to receive my order?
Delivery times depend on the shipping method chosen and your location. Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days, express shipping 2-3 business days, and overnight shipping 1 business day for domestic orders. International orders may take 7-21 business days.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to many countries worldwide. International shipping options and costs vary by destination. You can check available shipping methods and costs at checkout.
How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the carrier's website.
What if my package is lost or damaged?
If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll work with you to resolve the issue and send a replacement if necessary.
